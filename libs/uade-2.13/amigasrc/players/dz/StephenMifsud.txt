    Player info and history:  Darius Zendeh modules are thought to be and old
			prototype of the actual MARK II editor. This old
			version was coded by the composer himself -
			Darius Zendeh.

                              MARK II Sound System, an imporoved and released
                              editor, was then created by Andreas Scholl (and
                              little by Darius Zendeh) and was then published
                              by Cachet Software. The new MARK II modules are
                              reckognizable by the .ZADS89. text string about
                              $220-$320 bytes from the module start, and this
			can be played by MARK_II noteplayer Deliplayer.
			Since this editor was released (by Cachet Soft-
			ware), many modules are found in this format.


                  Functions:  Subsongs, Tempo, Mod type identification.

             Future Updates:  Volume and balance control.

                 Known bugs:  If you stop the mod and click on next/prev
                              song, noise can be generated. ( no crashes
                              though :) )
