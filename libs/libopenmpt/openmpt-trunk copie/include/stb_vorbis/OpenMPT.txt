(commit e713a69f1ea6ee1e0d55725ed0731520045a5993)

Modifications:
 *  Apply commit f32854c809ab261c07f09f5f27435daadccc2728 of branch
    https://github.com/rygorous/stb/tree/fix_vorbis_ilog (
    https://github.com/rygorous/stb/commit/f32854c809ab261c07f09f5f27435daadccc2728
    ).
